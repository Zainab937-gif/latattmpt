Welcome to this space. I built this blog to share ideas that don't fit anywhere else — half-formed thoughts, technical deep dives, and everything in between.

## Why Dark?

Not just the theme. There's something about embracing the uncomfortable, the unpolished, the raw — that's what this blog is about. No corporate speak. No fluff.

## What To Expect

I write about:
- **Technology** — tools I use, things I build, mistakes I make
- **Ideas** — philosophy, systems thinking, mental models
- **Process** — how I work, how I think, how I break things
